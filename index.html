About

The toolkit chARp Molecular Builder provides an augmented reality environment for building and simulation of molecules. chARp Molecular Builder was designed to enhance the normal workflow of a computational chemistry research with immersion into the data, without the drawback of abandoning the real world like in fully virtal reality environments. In particular, chARp Molecular Builder encourages a hybrid setup of workstation and AR device.
